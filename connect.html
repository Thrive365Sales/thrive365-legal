Connect to Thrive 365 QBO Integration

This application is for internal use by Thrive 365 only. Connection is managed by authorized personnel.

How to Connect

The Thrive 365 QBO Integration uses OAuth 2.0 to securely connect to your QuickBooks Online account. To establish or re-establish a connection:

  1. Contact the Thrive 365 administrator to initiate the connection process.
  2. You will be redirected to Intuit's authorization page to grant access.
  3. Review the requested permissions and approve the connection.
  4. Once authorized, the integration will begin processing data automatically.

What We Access

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, the Thrive 365 integration will have access to your accounting data as described in our Privacy Policy. This includes customer, vendor, invoice, payment, and financial data used for internal business automation.

Security

  • All connections use OAuth 2.0 authentication via Intuit's official authorization servers.
  • Your QuickBooks credentials are never stored by our application.
  • You can revoke access at any time through your QuickBooks Online account settings.
