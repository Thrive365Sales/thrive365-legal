Disconnect from Thrive 365 QBO Integration

Your QuickBooks Online account has been disconnected from the Thrive 365 integration.

What This Means

  • The Thrive 365 application will no longer access your QuickBooks Online data.
  • All automated workflows connected to your QBO account have been stopped.
  • Any data previously retrieved will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Request Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete any data that was previously retrieved from your QuickBooks Online account, please contact us at Patrick.Kerim@thrive365sales.com.

Reconnect

If you disconnected by mistake or would like to reconnect, visit our connection page.

Manage App Access in QuickBooks

You can also manage connected applications directly from your QuickBooks Online account:

  1. Log in to QuickBooks Online
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon) > Account and Settings
  3. Navigate to the "Other preferences" or "Apps" section
  4. Find "Thrive 365" and manage or revoke access
