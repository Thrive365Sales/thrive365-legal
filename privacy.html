Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Thrive 365 ("we," "us," or "our") operates a private internal integration with Intuit QuickBooks Online ("QBO") for business automation purposes. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information accessed through our QBO API connection.

1. Information We Access

Through our QBO API integration, we may access the following types of data from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Customer and vendor information (names, contact details, addresses)
  • Invoices, estimates, and sales receipts
  • Payment records and transaction history
  • Bills, expenses, and purchase orders
  • Product and service listings
  • Account and financial summary data
  • Employee information
  • Journal entries and general ledger data

2. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed through the QBO API is used exclusively for:

  • Internal business automation and workflow management
  • Generating reports and dashboards for internal use
  • Synchronizing data between QBO and our internal business systems
  • Streamlining invoicing, billing, and payment tracking

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data retrieved from QBO is processed and stored within our secure internal systems, including Microsoft 365 (SharePoint and OneDrive) environments.
  • We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all API communications.
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel within Thrive 365.
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication is used for all QBO API connections, and credentials are stored securely.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your QBO data with any third parties. Data accessed through the QBO API is used solely for internal business operations as described in this policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain data accessed from QBO only for as long as it is needed for the business purposes described above. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted from our systems.

6. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Information about what data we have accessed from your QBO account
  • Deletion of any data we have stored from your QBO account
  • Disconnection of our API access to your QBO account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
